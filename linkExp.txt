Master Thesis project: Unraveling Cancer Using Big Data and High Performance Computing
The objective of this thesis is to compare the occurrence of reads common across patients and or
cancers. To our knowledge this is the first attempt to compare and store common reads across dif-
ferent cancers and patients. In order to compare reads across multiple patients, one needs to create
a database to store the reads. Due to the scale of this data (600 MB to 300GB) high-performance
computing systems are required. We have investigated the suitability of a schema-less or NoSQL
database MongoDB with a traditional relational database MySQL and find that MongoDB is bet-
ter suited for the specific questions on hand.
Sentiment Barometer in Financial News

(FNR) for a duration of 3 years (2012-15). Contribution Chapter 3 Sentiment Barometer and

Shopping Optimisation Problem (ISOP)., which deals with multiple products to be acquired from
